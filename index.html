Welcome to my website again. I am Yu Jie Wu specialising in mechanical/automation engineering. My most important characteristic is curiosity. I always keen to understand either the methodology or the concept behind the scenes. It sounds quite nerdy. I admid it. However, who cares. I mean, this is my website. If you dislike it, create your own one. You're still there? Cool. Then let's begin the boring part.

I am a typical Asian, born in Taipei, Taiwan and in a "modern" chinese family, which is actually quite traditional. I can still remember how terrible I was requested to remember the 9X9 chart as I was in my third grade winter vacation. hanging out with my friend had been basically a taboo topic till I got into my fresh year in National Tsing Hua University. and... I messed up my fresh year and ended up with a GPA 2.3/4.3, which was still good enough to keep me in NTHU.


My life got changed dramatically after I met a guy called Wayne, who was the top 3 student at my school and is a all time intellegence. He got hired by a Japanese company before graduated. What a stunning accomplishment. We got along quite well. He is such a humble guy. Even though I kept helping him out with his stupid thermal dynamic problems, he was still patient enough to hold his temper.

Eventually, I graduated with a average grade of +80% and luckily got the admission of the university of Manchester. I finished 5 group projects, 1 interdiciplinary research project and 1 product development project with international team members and the academic/ industrial support. I finished my MSc. with a final grade +60(without merit) and I came back to Cologne to finishe a C1 level german intensive course at Carl Duisburg Centrum.

Now, I am currently located in Taiwan and looking forward to starting my career at 3M Taiwan as a Automation Engineer in O2 program. You may have interests to know I am also a black belt Judo Player, ameteur drummer, hobby photographer, and APP developer.

It is time to wrap up!
